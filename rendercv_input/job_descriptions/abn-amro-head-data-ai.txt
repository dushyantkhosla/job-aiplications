About the job
As Head of Data and AI at ABN AMRO Verzekeringen (AAV) in Zwolle, you will lead our transformation into a data- and AI-driven entity. In this role, you will shape the future of our company and be a supporter of change at the heart of our organisation. Doing so, you will help us deliver faster service, make smarter decisions and provide real impact for our customers.

What you are going to do

ABN AMRO Verzekeringen is a joint venture between ABN AMRO Bank and NN Group. This unique position combines the distribution strength of ABN AMRO Bank with the scale, insurance expertise and innovation power of NN Group. In this role, you define how data and AI accelerate our strategic ambitions and translate them into practical results. From faster claims processing to smarter pricing and better risk insights, your work improves customer experience while strengthening performance. As a member of the Management Team, you influence decisions at the highest level and represent ABN AMRO Verzekeringen within the broader NN Group data and AI ecosystem.

Key responsibilities:

Develop and deliver the organisation-wide data strategy and AI roadmap, translating strategic priorities into measurable improvements in customer journeys, operational efficiency and financial outcomes
Build and lead a central Data and AI domain of approximately 28 FTE by integrating teams from Commercial & Operations, Finance and Data Delivery into one aligned structure with clear governance, priorities and performance standards
Oversee and further develop BI reporting and management information as an integral part of our data‑driven decision‑making framework
Lead enterprise‑wide data management, covering data architecture, lifecycle and metadata management, with strong foundations in modern data‑warehousing, lakehouse technologies and data virtualisation
Identify, prioritise and scale high-impact AI use cases, including generative AI applications such as automated claims documentation and decision support, guiding them from pilot to full operational implementation
Establish and safeguard data governance, data quality standards and responsible AI frameworks in close collaboration with IT, Risk, Compliance and the Data Protection Officer, ensuring regulatory compliance and ethical application
Act as a strategic MT sparring partner and as a representative towards our shareholders at NN Group and ABN AMRO Bank, supervisory authorities and other stakeholders, aligning interests and securing support for key Data and AI investments
